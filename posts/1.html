My Pashto Couplets Collection

Mahan Marwat Dec 10, 2016

A collection of my favorite Pashto couplets.

Khalq wayee marree hes kawlay nashe

Za da mrro stargo da lasa dar pa dar yam --

Janatuna de qurban sha sta kusay na

Da ta dar mata afzal de da Kabay na

Pa zrrgi yeh zama stray charay wakrray

Sadaka shma da sta wrrukay khwlay na -- Afgar Bukhari

Che tola shpa da cha pa wrh yeh zharrl

Da ba mayan wo nor pa sayeh zharrl -- Afgar Bukhari

Da dedan khairat de krra nawakhta kezhe

Tabasum Marwat da lhray musafar yam -- Tabasum Marwat

Larr sha Darda yar ta sar titi okrra

De sara Pakhto naze Gherat naze -- Dard

Okrra lag khatir zama da sro stargo

Dair de yam daghlay pa de mrro stargo

Za Khatir hagha yam, Yar badal shwalo

Na goree os mata pa zarro stargo -- Khatir

Tazhee garzee Ashqan da toro stargo

Khudaya okrray yo baran da toro stargo -- Ayub Sohail

Dasay rata goray laka na chay rata goray

Negh main la zrrhgi wazee kazhn che rata goray -- Khatir

Da shbnam ghwanday fitrat larama Darda

Che Pakhto ta kisa oraseda oor shom -- Dard

Khashwala dera che inqar de oko

Zama da warra umar kar de oko

Khalaq che nakha walee goree warta

Ta che makh pat ko no guzar de oko -- Khatir

Loyh daryab da menay nashe pa warrh zrrh

Shuma yara, bya da sta ghunday pa na zrrh

Che pa khulh kawee yawa aw pa zrrh bala

Da hagha sara ba mena karray pa sa zrrh -- Khatir

Na zama day, na zama wo, na da wayam che ba mashe

Ae Khatira Khudai gawa de, bia main hum pa zrrh waray gee -- Khatir

Dobara de ratlh neshta pa dunya

Nan de war de, ka darogh krray ka rekhtya

Tair sa'ath pa mesal marray da lahad de

Marr cha naday zamday karray pa jarra --

Ka de nait we da Kabay da jorra walo

Da bewasa insan zrrh jorr ka, Kaba da --

Pa mazar main dasay ghlay ma teray zha

Toray khawray kho awaz kawalay nashe --

Da lewano ghwanday main tal, da ghoto shmar karray de

Ta sh khabar yeh, ma de sumra intezar karray de --

Aw ka turo jang wo, maba wo karray

Da nasib jang de, war khata walarr yam --

Khalaq wayee pa lido ke yeh qarar de

Ma Rehman che yar lidhlay beqarar yam -- Rehman Baba

Da sta meenay sa nadara ruswayee da

Dewana pa zmaka prewata bewakhta -- Dewana Sahib

Umar che tair she, Janan hair she

Ta de kasam we, che ma hair nakay meena --

Tar tar prot de, cha ratol naka Sahila

Pakhtoon watan ghrewan da lewanee de -- Sahil

Tor kho Darda tor de, sumra khwand kawee

Toray stargay, toray zalfay, tor barrn -- Dard

Sta ghamuna main pa zrrh banday dranh dranh

Laka karri we pa ghrh banday dranh dranh -- Afgar

Che khatir de pakay khawray she Khatira

Dumra makawa Khatir da cha da stargo -- Khatir

Da mayan marawartya kho da bachee marawartya da

Ka yozal warta musqay shway, Tabasum Marwat pa khwla da -- Tabasum Marwat

Gull ta kho har cha stargay war warray zhe

Zaka darta goram khafa kezha ma -- Dard

Dard darta jolai pa las newalay da

Har zulam kawa kho marawrega ma -- Dard

Dard che kala rashe ma jarra ta rasa we

Da sta khumaray stargay ma sehra ta rasa we -- Dard
